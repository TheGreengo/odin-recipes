Lasagna Recipe

  • Recipes

    • Here's a great lasagna recipe:

    Ingredients:

    • Marinara sauce (1 jar)
    • 1 lb ground beef
    • 1 Tablespoon italian seasoning
    • 1 large white onion
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 1 Jar Bechemel Sauce
    • 1 box lasagna noodles
    • Salt and pepper
    • Olive Oil
    • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

    Steps:

    1. Cook the meat in a large pan, adding in the tablespoon of italian seasoning as well as salt and pepper to taste
    2. Set meat aside, and dice the onion
    3. In a tablespoon of olive oil, cook the onion until translucent
    4. Add the garlic cloves (crushed or finely chopped) to the onion and keep cooking for thirty seconds
    5. Add the meat back in, as well as the jar of marinara sauce
    6. Cook 2-3 minutes on low heat
    7. In a 9x13 dish, layer about a half cup of your meat sauce, then a half cup of the bechemel sauce, then add a layer of dry lasagna noodle
    8. Repeat until all noodles and sauce have been used
    9. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese and bake at 325 f for 1 hour
    10. Let cool, and enjoy!!!
