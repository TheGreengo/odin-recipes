No-Bake Cookies Recipe

  • Recipes

    • Here's a great No-Bake Cookie recipe:

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups white sugar
    • 1/2 cup butter
    • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 1 pinch of salt

    Steps:

    1. In a saucepan, combine the sugar, cocoa, butter, and milk and bring to a boil over medium heat.
    2. Boil for 1 minute, then kill the heat and stir in the remaining ingredients.
    3. Poor onto baking sheet in small, cookie sized "puddles".
    4. Let cool, and enjoy!!!
